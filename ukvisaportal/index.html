UK Visa Application Portal

The Home Office requires all prospective entrants to the UK intending to stay for 6 months or more to provide proof of their online activity to assess initial eligibility for entry. This information is processed into a United Kingdom Integration Score (UKIS) that determines your ability to live, work or study alongside UK nationals. This information will also be used once you are in the UK to ensure eligibility to remain.

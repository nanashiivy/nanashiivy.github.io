Please provide access to your online accounts

It is now mandatory that all visa applicants provide access to their online accounts as part of the application process. This data will be used to determine your social and cultural suitability of living in the UK. You will be awarded a UK Integration Score (UKIS) based on the data you provide, which will determine the outcome of your application.

Please select all relevant services where you maintain an active account. The more accounts that are linked together, the better your score will be.

Select accounts for your visa application

You must select at least 3 accounts that have been in use for 5 years or more.
